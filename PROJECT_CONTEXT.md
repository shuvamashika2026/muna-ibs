# MUNA Project Context

## 1. Product Name

MUNA

## 2. Product Positioning

MUNA is an AI-powered Brain–Gut Health Companion for people living with IBS.

It helps users understand the relationship between:

- food
- symptoms
- bowel movements
- stress
- sleep
- hydration
- medication
- daily habits

MUNA is not a diagnostic tool and does not replace doctors, dietitians, psychologists, or other qualified healthcare professionals.

## 3. Core Mission

Help people living with IBS better understand their personal brain–gut patterns and gradually build healthier, more disciplined lifestyles through compassionate guidance, personalised insights, and evidence-informed education.

## 4. Core Promise

MUNA does not promise a cure.

MUNA helps users:

- understand their own patterns
- identify possible triggers
- monitor progress
- build sustainable habits
- prepare useful information to discuss with healthcare professionals

## 5. Target Users

Primary users:

- adults living with IBS
- people unsure about their IBS subtype
- people struggling to understand personal triggers
- people affected by food anxiety, stress, poor sleep, unpredictable bowel movements, or reduced confidence

Initial focus:

- IBS-C
- IBS-D
- IBS-M
- IBS-U

## 6. Current Product Stage

MUNA is a working MVP.

Current capabilities include:

- Supabase authentication
- user profile
- meal logging
- symptom logging
- bowel movement logging
- Bristol Stool Scale
- hydration logging
- sleep logging
- medication tracking
- dashboard
- analytics
- gut score
- basic flare-risk logic
- AI chat
- AI health report

- Vercel deployment
